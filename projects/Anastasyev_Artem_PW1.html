What is a difference between W3C HTML5 and WHATWG HTML Living Standard?

W3C and WHATWG had been characterized as both working together on the development of HTML5. The W3C "HTML5" standard was snapshot-based (HTML5, HTML 5.1, etc.) and static, while the WHATWG "HTML living standard" is continually updated.

What is the minimal HTML page code?

The minimal HTML page code includes the "!DOCTYPE html", "html", "head", and "body" tags, along with the "title" tag inside the "head" section.

Comment on semantic and presentational markup. Why presentational markup is considered deprecated now?

Semantic markup focuses on the meaning of content, while presentational markup is about how content looks. Presentational markup is deprecated because it mixes structure with style, making it harder to maintain and less accessible for different devices.
